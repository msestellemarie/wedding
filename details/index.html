Details

Hotels: DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel by the Berkeley Marina.

We've blocked off a number of rooms at the DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel by the Berkeley Marina for our guests. When you call to make a reservation, be sure to mention that you're a part of our wedding ceremony. The DoubleTree is a short fifteen minute drive from our reception venue and overlooks the bay.

Restaurants: Too many to name!

There are too many great places to list. In the Berkeley and Oakland area, we recommend Drake's Dealership, The Butcher's Son, and Restaurant Cafe Valparaiso. In San Francisco, we recommend Farmhouse Kitchen Thai Cuisine, and VeganBurg San Francisco.

Things to do: More than you can shake a stick at.

The Bay Area is full of amazing sights and fun things to do. There's plenty of sights to be seen in San Francisco: The Golden Gate Bridge, Fisherman's Warf, and Twin Peaks to name a few. If you're up for a drive, we highly recommend you visit Muir Woods and Muir Beach. Also be sure to explore the Berkeley Botanical Gardens.
