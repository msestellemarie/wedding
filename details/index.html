Where should I stay?

We've blocked off rooms at the DoubleTree Berkeley Marina and the Claremont Club & Spa for our guests. They're both about a ten to fifteen minute drive to the wedding venue. You can book online here! There are also plenty of other hotels in the area if you're looking for something different.

What food should I try?

The Bay Area is jam packed with amazing food, so there are too many great restaurants to list. A few places we like in the Berkeley and Oakland area are Drake's Dealership, The Butcher's Son, and Jupiter Pizza. In San Francisco, we recommend Farmhouse Kitchen Thai Cuisine, 21st Amendment Brewery, and VeganBurg.

What's there to do?

The Bay Area is full of beautiful sights and fun things to do. In Berkeley, roam around Telegraph Ave. and visit the Botanical Garden. If you head downtown, you can visit Golden Gate park, explore China Town, or walk around Pier 39. If you're up for a longer drive, we highly recommend you visit Muir Woods and the Muir Beach overlook.

How do I find everything?

We've tried to do some of the planning for you. Check out the Map of Everything that highlights our favorite restaurants, tourist spots, and things to do in the surrounding Bay Area. It also includes our wedding venues, parking, and other important locations for the Big Day!
