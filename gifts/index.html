Gifts

Should I get you a gift?

That’s entirely up to you! We’re not expecting or demanding gifts from any of our guests. Your presence at our ceremony and reception are enough.

Where can I find your wedding registry?

If you'd like to get us a gift, we’ve put together a list on Blueprint Registry of the things we want most. We've loaded our registry with everything from a night of drinks and bowling in downtown San Francisco, to pie in the sky financial goals like a down payment on our first home. We’ve even included options to donate to some of our favorite charities.

Can I buy you something that isn't on the registry?

We’d really appreciate it if you would stick to the registry. We understand the desire to give us something special and unique, but there is no better gift than something we really want.

I bought you something. Should I bring it to the wedding?

First of all, thank you! We'd prefer it if you would send the gifts to our home address listed in our registry. We don't live in California anymore, and we'll be flying home afterwards. Any gifts we receive at the wedding will have to be shipped or checked on our flight.
