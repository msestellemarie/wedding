Gifts

Should I bring a gift?

That’s entirely up to you! We’re not expecting or demanding gifts from any of our guests. Your presence at our ceremony and reception are all that we really want.

Where is your wedding registry?

If you really feel inclined to give us something, we’ve put together a wedding registry on Blueprint Registry to help make things easier on you. We’ve loaded our registry with everything from pie in the sky financial goals to practical things we could use around the house. We’ve even included options to donate to charities on our behalf.

Can I buy you something not on the registry?

We’d really appreciate it if you would stick to the registry, but if you really feel this strongly about your gift idea, I suppose we can let it slide. I’m sure we’ll love it.
