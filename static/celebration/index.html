Celebration

When: Saturday, October 20th, 2018 at 1:00 pm.

Our wedding ceremony will begin at 1:30 pm. To make sure you have time to get situated, please arrive at the venue by 1:00 pm to be seated. We'll have drinks and snacks at the reception venue starting at 3:00 pm.

Where: The Redwood Grove at the UC Berkeley Botanical Garden & The Anna Head Alumnae Hall in Berkeley, CA.

We're excited to be married under the shade of the beautiful redwoods in UC Berkeley's Botanical Garden. If you have time, we encourage you to explore the rest of the Garden in the morning before or immediately after our ceremony. We'll host our reception in the Anna Head Alumnae Hall, located just outside the UC Berkeley campus.

Attire: Casual, semi-formal, and everything in-between.

Our wedding ceremony and reception have no strict dress code. More than anything, we want you to wear what makes you comfortable. Who knows, Pete might even show up wearing flip flops!

Food & drink: Dinner and drinks will be served during the reception.

We're planning on keeping things simple and delicious for our reception. There will be plenty of options for vegans and non-vegans alike. During the cocktail hour, there will be appetizers and drinks available. For the reception, there will be pizza, donuts and an open bar for partaking adults. Do you have any dietary restrictions? Let us know when you RSVP and we'll be happy to accomodate.

Transportation: Use our Lyft code to transport to and from our ceremony and reception venues.

There are a limited number of paid parking spots at both the ceremony and reception venues. To make things easier, we've created a Lyft code that you can use to transport to and from both venues, free of charge. We'll be emailing out more information leading up to the big day. If you have small children, don't worry. We've reserved a small number of parking spots at both venues just for you.
